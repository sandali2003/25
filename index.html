About Us

We hope to provide access to a variety of information and resources. Our mission is to support library education, promote literacy, serve as community hubs, encourage lifelong learning, and promote information literacy. NSBM Green University Library consists of 4 floors. Inside those floors there are study rooms, open study area facilities. There is also free wifi access.Overall, we help improve the intellectual literacy and social development of students who use the library. Library study rooms are set up with a comfortable and quiet environment for students to pursue their courses as well as other educational activities freely.

Open Study Areas

Open areas with comfortable and quiet environment

Ground floor

Open study area

The open study area on the ground floor is a floor equipped with tables, chairs and sofas with glass walls that allow the surrounding environment to be seen. There are two non air-conditioned study rooms. We have provided you with the convenience of studying in this open study area enjoying the sunlight and the beauty of the environment.


M floor

open study area

The open study area on the ground floor is a floor equipped with glass walls to allow students to study with tables, chairs and sofas. There are more than 20 bookshelves here and they can be studied in a wide range of subjects. There are two study rooms here. We have provided you with the convenience of studying in this open study area enjoying the sunlight and the beauty of the environment.

Second floor

open study area

The open study area on the second floor is a floor equipped with glass walls to allow students to study with tables, chairs and sofas. There are more than 20 booths here and they can be studied in a wide range of subjects. There is a study room here. We have provided you with the convenience of studying in this open study area enjoying the sunlight and the beauty of the environment.
